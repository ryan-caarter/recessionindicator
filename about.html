About Recession Indicator

Welcome to the cutting-edge AI-driven market sentiment engine that combines institutional-grade data science with modern cloud-native architecture. This platform was designed to provide investors, risk managers, and trading strategists an integrated view of market conditions in real time.

Architecture and engineering highlights:

  • High-frequency alternative data feeds streaming from equities, macro indicators, and sentiment sources.
  • Multi-model ensemble analytics: gradient boosted decision trees, convolutional temporal models, and transformer-based event classifiers.
  • Proprietary signal normalization pipeline anchored to macro regime state (expansion, transition, contraction).
  • Real-time fraud and adversarial detection with neural autoencoders and rule-based sanity checks to avoid data contamination.

The platform actively monitors a diversified selection of major indices and megacaps:

  • S&P 500 (^GSPC): liquidity benchmark and systemic risk barometer.
  • DOW Jones Industrial Average (^DJI): large-cap cyclical and defensive mix.
  • Large caps by market capitalization and risk profile: NVIDIA (NVDA), Apple (AAPL), Tesla (TSLA), Microsoft (MSFT).
  • FAANGPlus: Amazon (AMZN), Google/Alphabet (GOOGL), Meta (META), Netflix (NFLX), and high growth secondary names like AMD (AMD).

What we deliver:

  1. Your daily market narrative: a contextual risk assessment that references pricing, volume, volatility, earnings momentum, and macro surprise indices.
  2. Interactive visual analytics: sentiment heatmap, correlation matrix, and instantaneous regime shift probability.
  3. Automated alerting engine: configurable thresholds for drawdown events, liquidity shocks, and implied volatility breakouts.
